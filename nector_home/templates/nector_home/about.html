What is NECTOR?

NECTOR is an open source successor to the HECTOR project, both of which have been sponsored by the University of Pennsylvania School of Arts and Sciences. NECTOR is a powerful and expandable framework used in the collection, analysis, and sharing of security intelligence information.

NECTOR takes advantage of the functionality and stability of the Django framework, and incorporates a customizable database backend with a minimalistic frontend. The project is being developed without the use of JavaScript.

NECTOR's intuitive web-based frontend allows for easy data analysis, scan configuration, incident reporting, open source intelligence, and more.
